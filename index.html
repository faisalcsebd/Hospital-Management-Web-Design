About ISMC

The academic activities of Ibn Sina Medical College commenced through admission of 50 students in 2004-05 academic session. Through the years upto 2019, 15 batches of total 866 students have undertaken the course.

The college is affiliated with the University of Dhaka and recognized by Bangladesh Medical & Dental Council, the compitent Authority of the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare.

The students of 10th batch is now starting their internship in our own hospital. The admission procedure of session 2019-2020 is now on-line. The admission is based on merit keeping in conformity with Government order published and adopted by the Academic Council and adorned by the Governing Body of ISMC more
